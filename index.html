SRI MAANASAA DEVI

Maanasaa Devi Temple

Chittedu Village • Kota Mandal • Tirupathi District

Temple Highlights

Spiritual Serenity

Nestled in the peaceful Chittedu village, providing a meditative atmosphere for all devotees seeking the blessings of the Serpent Goddess.

Architectural Heritage

Traditional Dravidian design elements that reflect the rich cultural history of the Gudur division and the Tirupathi region.

Sacred Festivals

Special Poojas and Alankarams conducted during Nagapanchami and Navaratri, attracting devotees from all over Kota Mandal.

Location & Access

Situated in Chittedu Village, Kota Mandal. Easily accessible via the Chennai-Kolkata Highway near Gudur.
