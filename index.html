Temple History

The Sri Maanasaa Devi Temple in Chittedu village is a sacred destination steeped in spiritual significance. Maanasaa Devi, the sister of Vasuki and the daughter of Sage Kashyapa, is revered as the Goddess of Snakes and the protector against snake-related afflictions (Naaga Dosha).


Built with the devotion of the local community in the Gudur Division, the temple has become a focal point for pilgrims visiting the Tirupathi district. The temple architecture follows traditional Vastu Shastra, ensuring a flow of positive energy. Legend says that those who offer prayers here with a pure heart are shielded from poisons and find peace in their family lives.
